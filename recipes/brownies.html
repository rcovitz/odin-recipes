Brownie Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder

How to make Brownies

  1. Melt the butter on the stove
  2. Remove from heat
  3. Add remaining wet ingredients
  4. Beat in dry ingredients
  5. Spread batter in pan and bake
