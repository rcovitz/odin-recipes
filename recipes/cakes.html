Description

A rich super moist chocolate cake with a chocolate buttercream frosting. This is the best cake in the world!

Cake Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Vanilla
  • Boiling Water

How to make Burgers

  1. Stir dry ingredients in a mixing bowl
  2. Add in wet ingredients and boiling water
  3. Stir the mixture
  4. Pour the batter into cake pans
  5. Bake in preheated oven
  6. Make and apply frosting
